My Little Kingdom

Create your kingdom and keep it yours!

Can you get Happiness to 100?

Instructions

  • Create a kingdom and try to raise it's happiness to 100.
  • Hiring Clergy gives you 5 Clerics at the cost of 3 Knights.
  • Hiring Knights gives you 5 Knights at the cost of 3 Clerics.
  • Holding a feast adds 500 to your populace.
  • Hint: Happiness is related to the amount of clerics and knights you have in relation with your populace.
